About Us

NAS Daily is a popular social media brand that produces and publishes daily videos on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. The brand was founded in 2016 by Nuseir Yassin, who quit his job as a software engineer to travel around the world and document his experiences. The videos are typically around one minute long and cover a wide range of topics, including travel, culture, science, and social issues.

NAS Daily has gained a massive following and has become known for its engaging and informative content. The brand has also launched several spin-off channels, including NAS Daily Español, NAS Daily Arabic, and NAS Daily Kids. Additionally, NAS Academy was launched in 2020, offering online courses taught by experts in various fields.

Services

NAS Daily provides a variety of services, primarily focused on producing and distributing engaging and informative content through social media platforms. Some of the key services provided by NAS Daily include:

      Daily one-minute videos: NAS Daily produces and publishes daily videos on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. These videos cover a wide range of topics, including travel, culture, science, and social issues.
      NAS Academy: An online learning platform that offers courses taught by experts in various fields. The courses cover topics such as digital marketing, storytelling, photography, and more.
      Brand partnerships: NAS Daily partners with brands and organizations to create engaging and informative content that resonates with their target audience.
      Live events and speaking engagements: Nuseir Yassin, the founder of NAS Daily, is a popular speaker and has given talks at conferences and events around the world.
